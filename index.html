Hello There!

I am a Software Engineer from Vietnam. I'm currently attending college in the U.S. I love reading biographies about successful people because it gives me hope that if I work hard, take chances and be kind, someday I could become someone like Bill Gates, Arnold Schwarzenegger or Conan O'Brien.

Besides reading biographies, I also enjoy developing web/mobile applications. The inspiration for these side projects all came from the fact that I sometimes cannot find another application out there that serves my need. The idea of my current project, SimpleC, stems from my needs to find an application that combines the functionality of a to-do list and a calendar with drag-and-drop support.

In 2018, I decided to withdraw from Gettysburg College for 2 semesters to join a tech startup named Logivan as the first employee. This was an extremely risky decision at the time but in the end it was a life-changing experience. I had been able to absorb so much software engineering knowledge while applying it right away in our product. At Logivan, I was working with the smartest and most hard-working people in the industry, and thus, I had acquired the strongest of work ethic from them. Thanks to this experience, my internship at Ellucian was beyond successful. As of July 2019, Logivan had raised a total of $7.9 million in funding
